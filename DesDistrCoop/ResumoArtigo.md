# Computer-Supported Cooperative Work

Estudo dos conceitos que embasam o trabalho
cooperativo.  
Área de pesquisa multidisciplinar, destinada a examinar o design, a adoção e o uso do Groupware.  
[Artigo - Constructing CSCW: The First Quarter Century](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/256502991_Constructing_CSCW_The_first_quarter_century)

## Resumo do artigo

O CSCW se desenvolveu a partir de diferentes tecnologias computacionais, tentando solucionar problemas muito diversos.

Por conta disso continua surgindo cada vez que pesquisadores trabalham no desenvolvimento de diferentes tecnologias ou domínios de aplicação, e percebem que seus problemas são similares.

Reconhecer que esses problemas são relacionados e articular uma conceitualização sistemática dos seus inter-relacionamentos tem sido uma tarefa árdua que não pode ser considerada completa.

Bases de dados orientadas a transação facilitam trabalho cooperativo mas isolam usuários uns dos outros.  
Modelos de mensagens não permitem a execução prática das tarefas.  
Automação em escritório tinha sistemas rígidos que não levavam em consideração as reais formas de trabalho - necessário estudo com a etnográfica (técnica de coleta de dados antropológicos).

Pesquisadores do CSCW investigam múltiplas faces do trabalho cooperativo, desde a base, e tem criado grandes estudos em uma vasta gama de configurações.  

Devido ao comprometimento do CSCW em entender as práticas do trabalho cooperativo para influenciar o desenvolvimento de tecnologias colaborativas apropriadas, esta pesquisa tem pego trabalho prático real em seu contingente material. CSCW se tornou o principal local para estudos práticos.

O processo de construção do CSCW é contínuo, e pesquisadores se deparam com problemas e percebem que estes problemas já são relatados de alguma maneira relevante, ou poderiam se beneficiar de uma análise na perspectiva do CSCW.

## 1. What has been achieved, and what issues remain as challenges for the field

Seven articles that review a number of key threads of CSCW research.  
The problems addressed by CSCW research are quite diverse - they develop from a wide range of different computing technologies.  
In sum, CSCW emerged and continually emerges as researchers engaged with the development of different technologies or working with different application domains realize that they in their own work are faced with related problems and then seek to explore and articulate these problems.  
(emerged as a loose association of different research efforts)

## 2. The unity of CSCW: towards a CSCW research program

That the institutionalization of CSCW took place without a generally accepted research program is no accident.  
CSCW as a research area did not have a clear and well-articulated understanding that the disparate problems they experienced in different ways were of
the same kind.  
Recognizing these problems as being related and articulating a systematic conceptualization of their interrelationships, has been an arduous task that can hardly be said to have been completed.

Systems of online processing facilitates cooperative work by providing a shared digital representation.  
But didn't support actors in the 'articulation work'.
These systems are carefully designed to hide the presence of other users.  
Transaction oriented database might facilitate cooperative work but didn't support articulation work - this was a baseline insight for CSCW.

When the discourse model of message-handling technologies is applied to typical work
settings, ‘the model breaks down’. ‘Designers who draw pictures, software developers
who jointly write code, financial analysts who collaborate on a budget—they all need
coordination capabilities as an integral part of their work tools.

The attempts to computerize ‘office procedures’ under the banner of ‘offic
automation’ quickly turned out to be a flawed research program.  
Experimental systems were found to be inordinately rigid, and from early on workplace studies the realization emerged that the ‘office automation’ idea did not take into account the ‘situated’ character of working according to prescribed procedures.

What was slowly and hesitantly realized in this process was that this mix of technologies raised a set of fundamental issues that are all connected to the nature of cooperative work practices. **The system not only has to support the execution of ‘the theory’ built into the model, but also ‘the practice’.**

*CSCW is a bazaar that is being reorganized and hesitantly but cumulatively systematized as the CSCW research program developed and unfolded.*

The joining of forces in the formation of CSCW was conceived of in terms of <u>pre-existing schools of thought</u>. Frameworks originating in other research areas, for the purpose of addressing the problems of these areas, were imported:  
Distributed computing, distributed AI, human-factors engineering, software engineering, organization theory, industrial sociology, sociology of science, information systems design, psychology of learning, activity theory, distributed cognition, social psychology, cognitive science, ethnomethodology, etc

Thus, in the effort to ‘understand the nature and requirements of cooperative work with the objective of designing computer-based technologies for cooperative work arrangements’, building a corpus of ethnographic and similar workplace studies would be a key strategic goal in the CSCW research program.

### União do CSCW: Em direção a um programa de pesquisa CSCW

CSCW como área não tinha uma compreensão que os problemas experimentados de maneiras diferentes eram do mesmo tipo.  
Reconhecer que esses problemas são relacionados e articular uma conceitualização sistemática dos seus inter-relacionamentos tem sido uma tarefa árdua que não pode ser considerada completa.

Perceberam que bases de dados orientadas a transação (sistemas de processo online) facilitam trabalho cooperativo por meio de uma representação digital da pessoa, mas não permitem trabalho articulado - esses sistemas são feitos para que a presença de outros usuários seja escondida.

Os modelos de mensagem também são aplicáveis parcialmente, pois aplicados a alguns tipos de trabalho não funcionam. As pessoas devem ser capazes de se comunicar (parte teórica) e também de executar as tarefas (parte prática).

Exemplo: a tentativa de implementar "automação no escritório", tinha sistemas rígidos que não levavam em consideração o trabalho de acordo com os métodos "formais". Perceberam ser necessário a etnografia (técnica de coleta de dados antropológicos).

O CSCW está constantemente sendo organizado e aos poucos sistematizado. A união de forças para criação do CSCW vem de escolas criadas previamente em diferentes ramos.

## 3. Gauging the depth and scope of CSCW

CSCW researchers endeavoring to understand cooperative work and coordinative practices by investigating the multifarious world of cooperative work ‘from the ground up’, have over the years been building a large and rich corpus of workplace studies in a wide range of settings.  
They have tentatively—developed important elements of the conceptual framework that is required for CSCW to contribute constructively to the development of collaborative technologies.

One of the work domains that has attracted significant attention in CSCW workplace studies is what can loosely be termed ‘design work'.
Within this category of work, software engineering as a cooperative endeavor has been studied intensively.  
A distinctly different body of domain-oriented studies has investigated cooper
ative work in what has been categorized as ‘centers of coordination’.

There's a scarcity of CSCW investigations into the uses of large-scale coordination technologies such as ‘enterprise resource management’ systems.  
CSCW researchers have, very tentatively, developed conceptualizations of key aspects of cooperativework practices.

CSCW researchers are exploring classes of coordinative practices that are specific to cooperative work such as ‘mutual awareness’ among cooperating actors which was identified very early as a key issue in CSCW (ethnographic study).

CSCW research is beginning to achieve an understanding of the essential role of representational and coordinative artifacts in cooperative work.

In sum, the CSCW research program is obviously being realized. A conceptual framework is being built from the ground up, not in anything like a linear and swift fashion, but painstakingly, falteringly. But the required cumulative progression can be discerned.

### Entendendo a profundidade do CSCW

Pesquisadores do CSCW investigam múltiplas faces do trabalho cooperativo, desde a base, e tem criado grandes estudos em uma vasta gama de configurações.

Alguns estudos são nas áreas de 'design work' (engenharia de software como uma jornada cooperativa), 'centros de coordenação', entre outras.

## 4. Assessing the influence of CSCW

The CSCW literature offers insights into processes of organizing mindful heeding, that depends heavily on tools, technologies, and environments as integrating mechanisms for the social production of action.

Because of CSCW’s commitment to understand cooperative work practices for the purpose of influencing the development of appropriate collaborative technologies, CSCW research has to take seriously actual work practices in their contingent materiality.  
It is thus probably not unfair to say that CSCW has become the prime locus of practice studies.

### Compreendendo a influência do CSCW

## 5. Epilogue

This process is ongoing, as researchers faced with related problems arising from the design, implementation, and use of new technologies or types of system realize that *their problems are already addressed in some relevant* way or could benefit from being approached from a CSCW perspective

CSCW is continually being formed as an interdisciplinary research field endeavoring to understand cooperative work practices with a view to developing adequate computational technologies to facilitate cooperative work, mediate communication, and support the regulation of coordinative practices

### Epilogo

O processo de construção do CSCW é contínuo, e pesquisadores se deparam com problemas e percebem que estes problemas já são relatados de alguma maneira relevante, ou poderiam se beneficiar de uma análise na perspectiva do CSCW.

CSCW está constantemente sendo formado como um campo interdisciplinar para entender o trabalho cooperativo, visando desenvolver tecnologias computacionais adequadas.
